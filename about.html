Our Mission

To Inspire and Equip Artists, Designers and Teachers

Expoundry is the culmination of over 10 years of teaching art and design to grades kindergarten through adult education. The name Expoundry is two words combined which are expound and imagery. Our purpose is to explain art and design in detail with simplicity while using simple imagery. Our goal is to inspire makers to create, and equip teachers to inspire future makers. Our tools encourage curiousity and inspiration to remix the world around you, fostering a mindset that enjoys experimenting with different possibilities, being persistant and taking creative risks while using a purposeful methodology.

Providing A Complete Art and Design Curriculum for Teachers

There are many art and design resources to choose from when looking for a solid curriculum. It can be daunting for creative arts teachers to find, compile and scaffold a curriculum that is clear, concise and differentiated. Our tools provide projects that teachers can use to teach traditional art, graphic design, multimedia, design thinking and technology integration with the arts. Our goal is to provide an extensive curriculum that will reach all students and give teachers a well organized structure that can be used at any level.
