Hi, Nice to Meet You!

Expoundry is the culmination of over 10 years of teaching art and design to grades kindergarten through Adult Education. Our goal is to inspire makers to create, and equip teachers to inspire future makers. There are many different ways to learn and create good design. Our tools encourage curiousity and inspiration to remix the world around you, fostering a mindset that enjoys experimenting with different possibilities, being persistant and taking creative risks.

Providing A Complete Art and Design Curriculum for Teachers

There are many art and design resources to choose from when looking for a solid curriculum. It can be daunting for creative arts teachers to find, compile and scaffold a curriculum that is clear, concise and differentiated. K-12 art education has reletively remained the same since it started. The curriculum usually consists of understanding the elements and principles, drawing from life and learning from previous art masters. What is often left out, is instructing teachers how to teach Graphic design, multimedia, design thinking and integrating technology with the arts. In any given classroom, teachers are faced with large class sizes, with students who have low attention spans and don't take the arts seriously. It is common to have a vast range of student levels ranging from special education to gifted. There is a huge discrepancy of resources with some schools having a large budget with plentiful technology, but others with an very low budget. Our goal is to provide an extensive curriculum that will reach all students and give teachers the tools they need to succeed.
